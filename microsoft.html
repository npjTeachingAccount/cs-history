In 1975 Paul Allen and Bill Gates Co-Founded Microsoft and are considereded to be at the forefront of the PC Boom.

While Microsoft became arguably the greatest company in the world in 1998 Microsoft was charged by the U.S Government for anti-trust laws. Claiming that Microsoft was using their dominance to drive out competitors. 2001 Microsoft joined the video game industry demonstrating their dominance once again rivaling Sony.
